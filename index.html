Project Overview & Market Position

New Murabba Development Company (NMDC), wholly owned by Saudi Arabia's Public Investment Fund, is executing this transformational urban development at the intersection of King Salman Road and King Khalid Road, encompassing the al-Malqa and al-Qirawan districts.

Mukaab: Architectural Centerpiece

The 400-meter cubic structure represents the world's largest single building by volume (60 million cubic meters), designed by AtkinsRéalis incorporating traditional Najdi architectural elements with advanced holographic display technology. The structure will serve as the project's commercial and cultural nexus.

Regulatory Environment Assessment

New Investment Law Implementation (February 2025)

Regulatory Framework
Saudi Investment Law & Foreign Investment Opportunities

Regulatory Reform: Royal Decree No. M/19 (August 11, 2024) establishes equal treatment framework for foreign and domestic investors, transitioning from permit-based to registration-based system effective February 7, 2025.

Regional Headquarters (RHQ) Program

Requirement Specification Compliance Timeline Incentive Period
Minimum Employment 15 FTE 12 months 30 years
Executive Presence 3 C-Level 6 months 30 years
Corporate Tax Rate 0% Immediate 30 years
Saudization Exemption 100% Immediate 10 years

Market Response: 184 multinational corporations relocated regional headquarters to Saudi Arabia in H1 2024, demonstrating program effectiveness and competitive positioning.

Nitaqat Localization Updates

2024 regulatory updates classify foreign investors as Saudi nationals for Nitaqat purposes, significantly reducing localization compliance burdens while maintaining sector-specific requirements for engineering (30%), accounting (40%), and project management (40%) roles.
