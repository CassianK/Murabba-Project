Regulatory Framework
Saudi Investment Law & Foreign Investment Opportunities

Regulatory Reform: Royal Decree No. M/19 (August 11, 2024) establishes equal treatment framework for foreign and domestic investors, transitioning from permit-based to registration-based system effective February 7, 2025.

Regional Headquarters (RHQ) Program

Requirement Specification Compliance Timeline Incentive Period

Market Response: 184 multinational corporations relocated regional headquarters to Saudi Arabia in H1 2024, demonstrating program effectiveness and competitive positioning.

Nitaqat Localization Updates

2024 regulatory updates classify foreign investors as Saudi nationals for Nitaqat purposes, significantly reducing localization compliance burdens while maintaining sector-specific requirements for engineering (30%), accounting (40%), and project management (40%) roles.
